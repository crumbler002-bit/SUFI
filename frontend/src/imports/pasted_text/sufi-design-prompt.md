SUFI is a restaurant discovery and reservation platform where:

Customers can:

discover restaurants

search by cuisine/location

view restaurant details

reserve tables instantly

see trending restaurants

receive recommendations

leave reviews

Restaurant owners can:

register their restaurant

manage reservations

view analytics

manage tables and bookings

In the future, SUFI will also include an AI assistant for restaurant discovery and booking, so the visual language must reflect a modern AI SaaS product.
